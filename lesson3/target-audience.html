Target Audience

The target audience are small business owners and professionals in the Great Lakes community. These are individuals who are either native to the Great Lakes community or are moving within our city boundaries. Generally, these are either small-to-medium business owners and professionals who seek to take advantage of the network of businesses and professionals that are native to the area, gaining resources from that group and aiding in the betterment of the community as a whole.
